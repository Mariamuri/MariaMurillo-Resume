After 6 months I got moved up to to-go's which was very different compared to what I was doing in the back. I had to carry big heavy trays with one hand which was out of my comfort zone and I doubted myself but I have gotten used to it. Another 6 months later I got moved up to expo which consists of making sure the tables get their food on time and get their orders made correctly. I have been doing expo for a year now and I say I definitely have seen an improvement in the speed I work in. I am a hard working person and am always striving to do better.
